### Summary
- Negative emotional memories fade faster than positive ones

### Details
- the memory may remain, but the emotional component will fade.

### References
- [[4x - Reading and Learning/41 Books/41.02 Book reviews/The Idiot Brain - Dean Burnett\|The Idiot Brain - Dean Burnett]]

### Quotes
> How about the fading-affect bias,27 where emotional memories for negative events fade more quickly than positive ones. The memories themselves may remain intact, but the emotional component of them can fade with time, and it seems that, in general, unpleasant emotions fade faster than nice ones.
