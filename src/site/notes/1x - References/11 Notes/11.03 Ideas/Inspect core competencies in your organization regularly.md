### Summary
- Know the most important things to your organization, and inspect them on a regular basis

### Details
- Let the team know that this is important and valued to the organization

### References
- [[4x - Reading and Learning/41 Books/41.02 Book reviews/The Wisdom of the Bullfrog - William H. McRaven\|The Wisdom of the Bullfrog - William H. McRaven]]

### Quotes
> 1.Identify the core competencies within your organization. 
> 2.Develop a plan to inspect these areas on a regularly scheduled basis. 
> 3.Show up during an inspection to ensure the rank and file understand that you, the leader, value the process and their efforts.
